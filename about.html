About GreenzoneLk

Passionate about plants, committed to sustainability, dedicated to your success

Our Story

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Gampaha, Sri Lanka, GreenzoneLk began as a small family business with a big dream: to make beautiful, healthy plants accessible to everyone across the island.

What started as a passion project in our backyard greenhouse has grown into Sri Lanka's most trusted online plant retailer. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of living with plants.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of happy customers across Sri Lanka, delivering not just plants, but the knowledge and support needed to help them thrive.
